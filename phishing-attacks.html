Understanding Phishing Attacks

Posted on September 15, 2024

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack that tries to deceive individuals into divulging sensitive information by masquerading as a trustworthy entity.

How Phishing Works

Phishing attacks typically use emails, websites, or messages that look legitimate to trick users into providing personal information.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Be cautious with unsolicited emails or messages.
  • Verify the authenticity of requests for sensitive information.
  • Use security software and keep it updated.
